English Summary

AntiScamer Screen Share Monitor detects when a website starts a WebRTC call or requests screen-sharing.  
It sends instant alerts to your Telegram bot.  
No data collection, no video capture ‚Äî only event detection.

The extension automatically:
‚Ä¢ tracks video calls
‚Ä¢ captures screen sharing requests
‚Ä¢ understands when you click "Share screen"
‚Ä¢ sends a warning to two emergency contacts you specify in the extension in Telegram.

 The extension does NOT see your screen, does NOT record video, and does NOT collect personal data. 
It only detects when you start a screen sharing session.

 How to use:
1. Install the extension;
2. Go to the Telegram bot, @AntiScumersBot (https://t.me/AntiScumersBot), and write the command /start.
You need to write it to both yourself and your emergency contacts, otherwise the alarm message will not be sent to your emergency contacts.
3. Enter the Telegram ID of your emergency contacts in the extension. If you don't know how to use it, use the Telegram bot - @username_to_id_bot (https://t.me/username_to_id_bot).
4. If you try to display your browser screen, your emergency contacts will receive an instant warning via Telegram.
